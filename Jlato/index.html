What is gelato?

Gelato is an Italian-style ice cream that is lower in fat and richer in flavour compared to regular ice cream.

Why choose J-lato?

We are extremely passionate about gelato and sorbet, to the extent that we import all of our ingredients and equipment directly from Italy. We offer a wide variety of flavours, our products are gluten-free and per request, can be lactose and sugar free as well. We cater for events and parties and we also provide freezers for our products to be stored in at any location.

What we offer

Catering for events and parties - both indoor and outdoor.

We supply freezers/display freezers for our products.

We offer confectioner services - Our chef will man a gelato/sorbet station, maintain the use of machinery and product distribution.

We provide stable, unique and quality products for distribution for various resturants and shops according to their requests.

We cater for clients who desire gluten free, vegan and lactose free options.
